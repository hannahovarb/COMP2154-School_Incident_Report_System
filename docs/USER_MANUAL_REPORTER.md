## Getting Started

The School Incident Reporting System allows you to report:
- **Bullying** - Harassment or bullying incidents
- **Maintenance** - Broken equipment, facilities issues
- **Safety** - Safety hazards or concerns
- **Lost Items** - Lost and found reports
- **Other** - Any other school-related concerns

---

## Creating an Account

1. Navigate to the application URL provided by your school
2. Click **"Sign up here"** on the login page
3. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Username**: Choose a unique username
   - **Email**: Your school email address
   - **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 6 characters)
   - **Role**: Select "Student" or "Teacher"
4. Click **"Sign Up"**
5. You will be automatically logged in after registration

![Signup Form](images/signup.png)

---

## Logging In

1. Enter your email and password
2. Click **"Login"**
3. You will be redirected to the report submission page

**Tip:** Check "Remember me" if using a personal device (not available on shared computers).

---

## Submitting a Report

1. After logging in, you'll see the **"Report an Incident"** form
2. Complete the following fields:

### Incident Type
Select the category that best describes your issue:
- Bullying
- Maintenance
- Safety
- Lost Item
- Other

### Location
Be specific! Examples:
- "Main Building - Room 201"
- "Cafeteria, near the salad bar"
- "Parking Lot, Section B"
- "Gymnasium, bleacher area"

### Description
Provide as much detail as possible:
- What happened?
- When did it happen?
- Who was involved? (if applicable)
- Any other relevant details

**Good Example:**
> "At approximately 10:30 AM during lunch break, I noticed a student struggling to open the locker near the water fountain. The lock appears jammed and the door won't close properly."

**Poor Example:**
> "Locker broken"

### Optional Photo
See [Uploading Photos](#uploading-photos) section below.

### Anonymous Reporting
See [Anonymous Reporting](#anonymous-reporting) section below.

3. Click **"Submit Report"**
4. You'll see a success confirmation message

## Anonymous Reporting

If you prefer to report without revealing your identity:

1. Check the **"Report anonymously"** checkbox before submitting
2. Your name will appear as "Anonymous" to administrators
3. **Important**: If you want to receive status updates, provide a valid email address during registration (it won't be publicly visible)

**When to use anonymous reporting:**
- If you're concerned about retaliation
- If you're reporting on behalf of someone else
- For sensitive issues where privacy is important

## Uploading Photos

Photos can help administrators understand the issue better.

### How to upload:
1. Click the upload area or drag and drop an image
2. Select a photo from your device
3. The image will appear with preview
4. You can **Change** the photo or **Remove** it before submitting

### Photo Requirements:
- **File types**: JPEG, JPG, PNG, GIF
- **Maximum size**: 5MB
- **One photo per report**

### Tips for good photos:
- Ensure adequate lighting
- Capture the issue clearly
- Avoid including people's faces when possible
- Take photos from different angles if needed

## Tracking Your Reports

When logged in, you can track your submitted reports:

1. Click on your username in the navigation bar
2. Select **"My Reports"** from the dropdown
3. You'll see a list of all incidents you've submitted
4. Each report shows:
   - Incident type
   - Status (Pending, In Progress, Resolved)
   - Date submitted
   - Quick actions

### Status Meanings:
| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Pending** | Report received, waiting for review |
| **In Progress** | Being addressed by staff |
| **Resolved** | Issue has been addressed |
